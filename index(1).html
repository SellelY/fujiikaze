HELP EVER HURT NEVER

Released May 20th 2020

Credits:

  • Arranged by [Sound Produced] - Yaffle (2)
  • Written by, vocals - Fujii Kaze

“HELP EVER HURT NEVER” is the first album by Japanese musician 藤井風 (Fujii Kaze) and is released on May 20, 2020. The album title is inspired by a phrase that Fujii's father used to play to Fujii, and Fujii says that “everything is summed up” in it. The album is characterized by a modern sound that absorbs the diverse essences ofR&B, hip-hop, and songs, as well as a unique lyrical and singing style that incorporates the Okayama dialect.
